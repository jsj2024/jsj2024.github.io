Sijia Jing

Ph.D. Student @ Nanjing University · LLM & Agent Safety Alignment
I am a Ph.D. student at Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Yang Gao, and a member of the Reasoning and Learning Research Group. My research focuses on the safety alignment of large language models and agentic systems, with an emphasis on multi-turn jailbreak robustness and trajectory-level risk modeling.
Nanjing, China · Ph.D. @ Nanjing University (2025–2030) · B.S. @ Henan University (2021–2025)
